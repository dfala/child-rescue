What we do

Rescue Missions

Child Rescue has been and is currently serving as
the fiscal sponsors of various tactical
teams who find, arrest, and prosecute traffickers.
These teams include individuals with years of real-
world experience and some of the most elite
training our government has ever created.

Education / Events

Youth Prevention. The backyard Boracast
leadership progrma is a year-long commitment that
brings teens from across North America together
to build a grassroots movement focused on
combating the sexual exploitation of children.

Tactical Training

Child rescue has partnered with some of the
nation's leading experts to educate law
enforcement on the world of trafficking. We have
created national trainings for law enforcment to
understand and effectively combat human
trafficking. 286 FBI and other officers have
already been trained.

Survivor Aftercare

When our teams complete each rescue
mission, the recovered victims are eventually
sent to qualified, pre-approved aftercare
facilities run by established organizations
such as the International Justice Mission.
